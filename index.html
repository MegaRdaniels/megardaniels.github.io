The home site for the comic "Rise of the Sky-Titans" and other indie projects. Based off the webcomic from The Duck Webcomic, after failing to bring down the man who murdered his mother in the court of law, an obsessive, struggling private detective, grieved by her death was suddenly murdered by a woman in black. When he was ressurected by the gift his mom gave him, he joins forces with an old flame, Stringy, a bubbly, zany superheroine with the power to transform into a werewolf with a mystical talisman; Pappy, a former US Army Ranger; and Snow White, a former assassin of the Golden Dragon Organization to not only bring down his Mom's murderer, but to also put a stop to a powerful, billionaire mob boss, Bullet, from using these Badges to reawaken the dead for world domination.'
